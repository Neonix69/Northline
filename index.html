About Northline

One studio to call. A full network working behind it.

Northline Productions is not a recording studio with a fixed roster and a single sound. It's a creative network — a curated collective of composers, producers, vocalists, instrumentalists, mixing engineers, mastering engineers, and sound designers, assembled fresh for every brief.

Clients work with one company, one point of contact, and one timeline — while Northline handles the casting, scheduling, quality control, and creative direction behind the scenes. The result is the flexibility of a global talent network with the accountability of an in-house team.

No two projects get the same team, because no two projects need the same team. That's the entire premise.
